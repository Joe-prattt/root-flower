About Chinese New Year Flowers

During Chinese New Year, flowers symbolize growth, prosperity, and good fortune. Popular choices include peach blossoms for luck, peonies for wealth, and chrysanthemums for longevity. Our bouquets are carefully designed to celebrate these traditions with a modern touch.
